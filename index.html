About Emily Lemonly

I started doing YouTube when I was 9—filming toy reviews, Harry Potter skits, and fun projects with my friends in my own little studio. I didn’t care about views—I just loved creating. Now, I’m a Computer Engineering major, and I still create—just in new ways. I make videos about calculus, tech demos, travel, and I’m starting to incorporate more of my engineering journey into my content. The topics have grown up with me, but the passion has always stayed the same.
